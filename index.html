    Volunteering The Future Looks Bright

    Feeling like there are countless pressing issues that no single program could make a marked difference? Our Volunteering initiative has proven to be a powerful tool for Trinidad Foundation to successfully serve our community and improve countless lives.

    Local Empowerment Change for the Better

    The Local Empowerment program is our longest running initiative, based on the premise that making a real difference is not only possible but vital. At Trinidad Foundation, Local Empowerment is a source of great pride for our Food Donation Center.

    The News

    We work hard to get our efforts noticed by the media and are so proud when this goal comes to fruition. Take a look at some of our latest coverage and help spread the word about all the amazing developments at Trinidad Foundation.

    About Us

    Founded in 2000, Trinidad Foundation has made tremendous impact thanks to the help of members and volunteers in the San Francisco community. The core of our work lies in the continuous passion and dedication we bring to the range of projects we take part in. Please join us by supporting our efforts to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.
